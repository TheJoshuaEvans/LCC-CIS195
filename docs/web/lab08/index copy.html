Welcome to Jeff's super pet foster agency!

Attention, Marvel fans and animal lovers alike! A crisis of epic proportions has erupted in the Marvel multiverse. Our beloved heroes are locked in an all-out battle against Dr. Doom and a host of other sinister villains. Amidst this chaos, their loyal pets are left without the care and attention they deserve. These extraordinary animals, who stand by our heroes in times of need, now find themselves in need of temporary homes.

Galacta Here!

Thank you for considering helping out all our amazing heroes! They can't focus on the fight if they are worrying about all their pets. You can be a hero too by helping take care of these little cuties!

We are calling upon compassionate souls to step up and temporarily adopt the pets of our superheroes. From Lucky the Pizza Dog to Goose the Flerken, these remarkable creatures require a safe and loving environment until their owners can return. By opening your hearts and homes, you will not only provide these pets with the care they need but also lend a helping hand to our heroes as they work tirelessly to restore peace to the multiverse.

Your kindness and support can make all the difference. Imagine cuddling up with Cosmo the Spacedog or taking care of Lockjaw, the majestic teleporting bulldog. These pets have unique abilities and characteristics that make them truly special. By temporarily adopting them, you become a part of their incredible journey and play a crucial role in the ongoing battle between good and evil. Join us in this extraordinary mission and let’s ensure that our heroes can focus on saving the multiverse, knowing their furry friends are in good hands. Together, we can be heroes too!
